TARGET AUDIENCE:

The target audience for Idaho Weather Now is travelers and locals seeking current weather conditions, as well as suggestions for outdoor activities in each area. The target audience is active, and interested in the outdoors.

Personas:

Visitor to Preston, Idaho
Visiting family looking for activities in Preston, Idaho. Family ranges in age from 2 to 42. The family is looking for an outdoor activity that is appropriate for the current weather conditions.
Resident
Adult male, 32 years old, observing the weekend weather, looking to see what local events may be of interest to him and his spouse.


Scenarios:

A student returning home to visit her family looks for weather conditions to make sure the drive is safe. She navigates to her hometown page and observes the weather, and makes her plans accordingly.
