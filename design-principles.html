Design Principles

Rule of Thirds




paypal.com

The rule of thirds relies on the eye's natural tendency to appreciate items in odd groupings. Paypal displays the rule of thirds by dividing the screen into three equal portions.

White Space




saatchiart.com

white space is needed to emphasize and focus the eye in one spot, instead of being lost in visual chaos. Saatchi effectively uses white space, leaving enough empty space for the viewer to appreciate a single picture at a time. This rule applies to home design as well---gallery wall style picture groupings can be visually overwhelming for this same reason.

Contrast




verizon.com

The rule of contrast applies to the readability of text on a screen. Verizon provides adequate contrast, which makes the site readable. When the value of a text and its background are too similar, the text can be hard to read. This can be overcome by increasing the size of the font, or increasing the contrast. However, extreme contrasts should be avoided. Bold colored text placed on a bold colored background from the opposite side of the color spectrum creates a vibrating effect, making text difficult to read.
